FAQ

The practical questions people ask first.

Who is this for?

Riders who want more from their hardware. VeloPlay is for v1 and v2 owners who want to break free from the stock software limitations and turn their tablet into a flexible, open training dashboard.

Do I need to be technical?

Not at all. If you're comfortable with basic settings, the self-install is straightforward. If you'd rather save time, our White-Glove Setup handles everything over a quick video call to get you riding immediately.

Does it replace the bike hardware?

The bike stays exactly as it is. VeloPlay is a software-layer upgrade. It simply unlocks the tablet's ability to display custom content and broadcast your existing sensor data to other devices via Bluetooth.

Is this affiliated with Peloton?

VeloPlay is an independent project. We are not affiliated with Peloton Interactive. We are an independent utility built by and for owners who want to extend the life and utility of their hardware.

Do I still get my Peloton metrics?

Absolutely. The core mission of VeloPlay is ensuring you never lose your data. Your cadence, resistance, and output stay pinned to the screen regardless of what app or game you are running.
